Introducing our new photo sharing app Stick-It! Stick It is a social media platform similar to Instagram, but with a unique twist: all uploaded to Stick-It Images are tagged with the location where they were taken and assigned to a group.

Join groups

Stick-It also lets you create and delete groups, which Simplifying the management of your content. Additionally you can all your photos and groups in a feed-like overview show. The groups displayed can always be replaced by one activated by just pressing a button at the top of the screen or be deactivated.
